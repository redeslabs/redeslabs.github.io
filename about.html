Who We Are

A global innovation company dedicated to bridging cultures and transforming industries.

At REDES, our name reflects our mission across languages. We're driven by the belief that the best solutions emerge when diverse perspectives converge. Our three pillars—Rethink, Detego, and Sophia—represent our comprehensive approach to solving complex global challenges.

With teams across multiple continents and expertise in AI, cultural preservation, and healthcare innovation, we deliver solutions that are not only technologically advanced but culturally resonant and accessible to all.
